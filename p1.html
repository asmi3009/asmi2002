Kathak is not just a dance form; it's an expression of my innermost emotions, a celebration of tradition, and a journey into the heart of Indian culture. The elegant spins, the mesmerizing footwork, and the storytelling through dance have woven a beautiful tapestry of artistry in my life.

📜 A Storyteller's Dance
